About
Welcome to Pacific Blue Apartments on the Strand!
Pacific Blue Apartments is the place to enjoy gorgeous ocean views and sunsets, sleeping to the sound of the surf, and making memories with your friends and family. Oceanfront on the Strand in Oceanside, we are literally steps to the sand. We are walking distance to the pier, harbor, and a fun downtown with restaurants, shops, a movie theater, craft breweries, and weekly farmers and sunset markets. Relax on the beach, stroll or jog along the waterâ€™s edge, go boogie boarding and surfing, or enjoy a bike ride.
We are also convenient to Disneyland, Legoland, San Diego Zoo and Safari Park, and Old Town. We are a 5 minute walk to the train station if you want to hop aboard to downtown San Diego.
Our property is completely non-smoking inside and outside.
